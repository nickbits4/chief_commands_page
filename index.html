Chief Beefs Available Twitch Commands

Command Description
!trivia Starts a Trivia question!
!answer [answer] Submits an answer for the active Trivia question
!bingo Generates a Bingo Card!
!bingorules Gives the rules of bingo!
!bingostatus Shows what bingo terms have hit so far
!currenthunt Shows what Pokemon are being currently hunted!
!discord Links to the Discord!
!probability [number of attempts] Shows what the odds of the Shiny being caught thus far is
!game Shows what the game we are playing!
